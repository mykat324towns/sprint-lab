# Session Analyzer

## Objective
Turn session data into the next training decision without emotional overreaction.

## Inputs
- rep times or estimates
- video observations
- consistency / drop-off
- subjective feel
- next-day response

## Rules
- Do not overfit one session
- Separate signal from noise
- Look for repeatability, not isolated highs
- Distinguish ceiling, fatigue, coordination instability, and underexposure

## Output
- What likely happened
- What not to conclude yet
- Most likely limiter direction
- Next action: progress / hold / regress
