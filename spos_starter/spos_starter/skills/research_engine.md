# Research Engine

## Objective
Find truth, reduce uncertainty, and extract only decision-relevant knowledge.

## Rules
- Separate evidence, inference, and speculation
- Prefer mechanism-level clarity over generic summaries
- Do not collect research that changes nothing
- For each source, answer:
  1. What does it support?
  2. How strong is the evidence?
  3. What programming decision does it change?
  4. What uncertainty remains?

## Output
- Key conclusion
- Confidence level
- What it changes
- What still needs testing
